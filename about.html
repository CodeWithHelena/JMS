About Us

Learn about our commitment to advancing academic research through open access publishing

Who We Are

FedponekIJRD (International Journal of Research & Development) is a leading peer-reviewed, open access journal dedicated to publishing high-quality research across multiple disciplines.

Founded with the vision of democratizing knowledge, we provide researchers, academicians, and professionals a global platform to share their innovative work with the scientific community worldwide.

Our rigorous review process ensures that every published article meets the highest standards of scholarly excellence while maintaining accessibility for readers everywhere.

Our Mission & Scope

Our mission is to foster the dissemination of knowledge by providing a platform for researchers to publish groundbreaking work that contributes to the advancement of science, technology, and society.

Scope: We publish original research articles, review papers, technical notes, and case studies across a wide range of disciplines including:

  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Medical & Health Sciences
  • Business & Management
  • Environmental & Social Sciences

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our commitment to academic excellence

Academic Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of scholarly rigor, transparency, and ethical publishing practices in every publication.

Collaboration

We believe great research happens through teamwork and shared knowledge across disciplines and borders.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform to meet the changing needs of the academic community and publishing landscape.

Security

We protect research data and intellectual property with enterprise-grade security measures and strict confidentiality.

Why Choose Us

Discover the advantages of publishing with FedponekIJRD

Global Indexing

FedponekIJRD is indexed in Google Scholar, Index Copernicus, Slideshare, Scribd, Academia, ResearchGate and many more prestigious databases ensuring maximum visibility for your research.

Peer Reviewed

An international, peer-reviewed, open access journal with a rigorous review process and a streamlined publishing workflow that ensures quality while maintaining efficiency.

e-Certificate

Provides individual eCertificates to each author, ensuring formal recognition of their authorship and contribution to published work for professional portfolios.

Online Submission

Authors can submit their papers at any time through the online submission system, ensuring a convenient and uninterrupted submission process 24/7.
