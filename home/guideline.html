Duties of Editors

Editorial Decision Making

Acceptance, rejection, or acceptance with modification decisions

Ensure fair play in the review process for all submissions

Ethical Oversight

Maintain confidentiality of submitted manuscripts and author information

Disclose and manage conflicts of interest appropriately

Participate actively in investigations of ethical issues

Duties of Reviewers

Review Process

Contribute valuable insights to editorial decision making

Provide prompt reviews within agreed timelines

Ethical Standards

Maintain confidentiality of manuscript content and review process

Uphold high standards of objectivity in evaluations

Properly acknowledge sources and intellectual property

Disclose and manage conflicts of interest

Duties of Authors

Research Standards

Adhere to high reporting standards with accurate representation

Provide data access and maintain retention as required

Ensure originality and avoid plagiarism in all submissions

Publication Ethics

Avoid multiple, redundant, or concurrent publication

Properly acknowledge all sources and prior work

Ensure appropriate authorship attribution for the paper

Duties of the Publisher

Our Commitment to Integrity

Editorial decisions are made independently of advertising, reprint and other commercial considerations. We support editors with legal/ethical review and maintain high standards for corrections and retractions when required.

Author Submission Guidelines

Authors should submit carefully proofread papers using the official template. This speeds processing and improves publication quality. Acceptance or rejection notifications will be sent to authors.

Download Paper Template (.docx)

Accepted Contribution Categories

Technical & Ethical Notes

Detailed technical notes and ethical considerations in research.

Analytical Reviews

Comprehensive and analytical reviews on scientific topics and advancements.

Original Research

Novel research contributions with significant scientific value.

Speedy Communications

Short, independent articles on ongoing research and preliminary findings.

Format & Submission Requirements

English is the supported language for all submissions.

Submit manuscripts in MS Word (.doc, .docx) format.

Maximum 100 double-column pages (exceptions may apply).

Conference/journal paper extensions are acceptable.

Author names in final manuscripts are indexed in the journal system.
