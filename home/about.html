About Journal Flow

Advancing knowledge through open access publishing since 2010

Our Mission

At Journal Flow, we believe that knowledge should be freely accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our mission is to democratize academic publishing by providing a platform where researchers can share their discoveries without barriers, while maintaining the highest standards of peer review and academic integrity.

Our History

2010 - Foundation

Journal Flow was founded by a group of researchers frustrated with traditional publishing barriers.

2014 - Expansion

Launched our first international partnerships and expanded to 50+ journals across disciplines.

2018 - Innovation

Introduced AI-powered peer review matching and automated publishing workflows.

2023 - Present

Serving researchers from 120+ countries with 15,000+ published papers and growing.
