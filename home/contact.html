Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about JournalFlow

How do I get started with JournalFlow?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for a free account on our platform. Once registered, you can explore our features, create a journal, or request a personalized demo from our sales team. We also offer onboarding sessions for new users.

What types of journals are supported?

JournalFlow supports all types of academic journals across various disciplines including sciences, humanities, medicine, engineering, and social sciences. Our platform is flexible and can accommodate different publishing workflows, review processes, and formatting requirements.

What are your pricing plans?

We offer tiered pricing based on your needs: Basic (free for small journals), Professional ($99/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing). All plans include core features, while higher tiers offer advanced analytics, custom branding, and dedicated support. Contact our sales team for a detailed quote.

How do I migrate my existing journal to JournalFlow?

We provide comprehensive migration support. Our team can help transfer your journal data, articles, reviewer information, and metadata. We offer automated migration tools for common platforms and manual assistance for complex migrations. Contact our migration team for a personalized migration plan.

Is my data secure on JournalFlow?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA). Data is backed up daily across multiple secure locations. We never share your data with third parties without explicit consent.
