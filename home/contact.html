Frequently Asked Questions

What is your submission process?

Our submission process involves manuscript submission, initial screening, peer review, revision, and final acceptance. The average time from submission to publication is 4-6 weeks.

Are there any publication fees?

We operate on an open access model with article processing charges (APCs) that vary by journal. Waivers are available for authors from low-income countries and institutions.

How can I become a reviewer?

You can apply to become a reviewer by creating an account and submitting your CV and areas of expertise. Our editorial team will review your application within 7-10 business days.

Is my research protected?

All submissions are protected by our privacy policy. We use secure servers and encryption to protect your intellectual property throughout the review process.

How long does peer review take?

The peer review process typically takes 4-6 weeks. We work with expert reviewers in your field to ensure timely and thorough evaluations.
