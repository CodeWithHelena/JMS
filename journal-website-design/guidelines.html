Guidelines

Comprehensive guidelines for editors, reviewers, authors, and publishers

Duties of Editor

Editorial Decision Making

  • Acceptance, rejection, or acceptance with modification decisions based on thorough evaluation
  • Ensure fair play in the review process for all submissions regardless of author background
  • Evaluate manuscripts based solely on intellectual content and scholarly merit

Ethical Oversight

  • Maintain confidentiality of submitted manuscripts and author information at all times
  • Disclose and manage conflicts of interest appropriately and transparently
  • Participate actively in investigations of ethical issues and research misconduct
  • Ensure the integrity of the peer review process through proper oversight

Duties of Reviewers

Review Process

  • Contribute valuable insights to editorial decision making through constructive feedback
  • Provide prompt reviews within agreed timelines to ensure efficient processing
  • Offer specific, constructive comments that help authors improve their work
  • Assess the originality, significance, and quality of the research

Ethical Standards

  • Maintain confidentiality of manuscript content and review process
  • Uphold high standards of objectivity in evaluations without personal bias
  • Properly acknowledge sources and intellectual property in reviews
  • Disclose and manage conflicts of interest before accepting review assignments

Duties of Authors

Research Standards

  • Adhere to high reporting standards with accurate representation of research findings
  • Provide data access and maintain retention as required by journal policies
  • Ensure originality and avoid plagiarism in all submissions
  • Report research methods and results honestly and transparently

Publication Ethics

  • Avoid multiple, redundant, or concurrent publication of the same work
  • Properly acknowledge all sources and prior work through proper citations
  • Ensure appropriate authorship attribution for the paper based on contribution
  • Disclose any potential conflicts of interest related to the research

Duties of Publishers

Our Commitment to Integrity

  • Editorial decisions are made independently of advertising, reprint and other commercial considerations
  • We support editors with legal/ethical review and consultation services
  • We maintain high standards for corrections and retractions when required
  • We ensure the long-term preservation and accessibility of published content
  • We protect the intellectual property rights of all authors and contributors

Submission Guidelines

Authors should submit carefully proofread papers using the official template. This speeds processing and improves publication quality. Acceptance or rejection notifications will be sent to authors via email.

Accepted Contribution Categories

Technical & Ethical Notes

Detailed technical notes and ethical considerations in research methodologies and practices.

Analytical Reviews

Comprehensive and analytical reviews on scientific topics, advancements, and current state of knowledge.

Original Research

Novel research contributions with significant scientific value and original findings.

Speedy Communications

Short, independent articles on ongoing research and preliminary findings for rapid dissemination.

Format & Submission Requirements

  • Language: English is the supported language for all submissions
  • Format: Submit manuscripts in MS Word (.doc, .docx) format only
  • Length: Maximum 100 double-column pages (exceptions may apply for comprehensive studies)
  • Extensions: Conference/journal paper extensions are acceptable with proper disclosure
  • Indexing: Author names in final manuscripts are indexed in the journal system
  • Template: Use the official journal template for consistent formatting
  • References: Follow APA or IEEE citation style as appropriate for your field
