FedponekIJRD is a leading international journal dedicated to the publication and dissemination of high-quality research. We provide a platform for researchers, academicians, and professionals to share their innovative work with the global scientific community.

Our commitment to excellence ensures that every published article meets the highest standards of scholarly rigor and contributes meaningfully to its respective field.
